Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of vehicles do you inspect?

We inspect a wide range of vehicles including used cars, new vehicles, commercial vehicles, and entire fleets. Our inspections cover both local and imported vehicles to ensure they meet roadworthy and mechanical standards.

2. How long does a typical inspection take?

Most inspections are completed within 24 to 48 hours, depending on the type and location of the asset. We aim for a fast turnaround without compromising on accuracy and detail.

3. Do you offer services outside Bulawayo?

Yes. We operate in Bulawayo, Harare, Gweru, and across Matabeleland North and South. For remote or special cases, contact us to arrange custom scheduling.

4. Can I book an inspection online or via WhatsApp?

Absolutely. You can reach us through our contact form, email, or directly on WhatsApp for fast responses and easy scheduling. Visit BhekaInspections.com (when live) for all contact options.

5. What sets Bheka-Vheneka apart from other inspection services?

We combine affordable pricing, quick turnaround, and expert technicians with transparent reporting and excellent customer service. Our goal is to help you make informed decisions with confidence.
